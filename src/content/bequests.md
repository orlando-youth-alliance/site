## How to include Orlando Youth Alliance, Inc. in my will

Your legacy is every life you touch.

The Easiest Way to Leave a Lasting Gift to Orlando Youth Alliance, Inc.

A bequest is as simple as it is powerful; with a few words in your will or trust, you can bring hope to hundreds of LGBT youth far beyond your lifetime. Chances are, even after you have made adequate provisions for your family and friends, you can have a bigger impact than you ever thought possible - and you don't have to be wealthy to do it. Best of all, when you make a bequest, you will have the good feeling of having done something significant for Orlando Youth Alliance, Inc.

### What You Need to Know

If you are already prepared to name Orlando Youth Alliance, Inc. in your will, you'll need a few simple facts about us:

**Legal Name and Address**: Orlando Youth Alliance, Inc. is a 501(c)(3) tax-exempt charitable organization, located at 255 S Orange Ave. Suite 104 #1985, Orlando, Florida 32801

**Our tax id**: Orlando Youth Alliance, Inc. Federal Tax ID: 59-3666953

**Gift declaration**: The gift can be declared in a number of ways. Here are some common ways to phrase your intentions:

#### Percentage of Estate:
> I give to Orlando Youth Alliance, Inc., currently of 255 S Orange Ave. Suite 104 #1985, Orlando, Florida 32801, ___% of my estate to be used in such a manner as the Board of Directors of Orlando Youth Alliance, Inc. shall, in its sole discretion, determines. 

#### Fixed Amount:
> I give to Orlando Youth Alliance, Inc., currently of 255 S Orange Ave. Suite 104 #1985, Orlando, Florida 32801, the sum of $____ to be used in such a manner as the Board of Directors of Orlando Youth Alliance, Inc. shall, in its sole discretion, determines. 

#### Specific Property:
> I give, devise, and bequeath to Orlando Youth Alliance, Inc., located at 255 S Orange Ave. Suite 104 #1985, Orlando, Florida 32801, description of property, located at exact location.

#### Residue of Estate (after probate costs and other expenses):
> I give to Orlando Youth Alliance, Inc., currently of 255 S Orange Ave. Suite 104 #1985, Orlando, Florida 32801, ___% of the rest and residue of my estate to be used in such a manner as the Board of Directors of Orlando Youth Alliance, Inc. shall, in its sole discretion, determines.
